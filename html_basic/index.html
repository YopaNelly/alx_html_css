level 2

1. Elf (Instance Variables): Imagine an Elf with a magical pouch that can store unique objects. Each Elf has its own pouch, representing instance variables. Just like an Elf's pouch, instance variables are specific to each object created from a class.

Level 3

2. Dragon (Methods): Visualize a Dragon with a repertoire of magnificent skills, such as fire-breathing, flying, and treasure-guarding. These skills represent methods in a class. Methods are powerful actions that objects can perform within a class.

Level 4

3. . Mermaid (Inheritance): Envision a captivating Mermaid, who possesses both fish-like qualities and human-like characteristics. The ability to inherit traits from different parent classes parallels the concept of inheritance in Python. Just as the Mermaid inherits traits from both fish and human classes, other classes can inherit attributes and methods from parent classes.
